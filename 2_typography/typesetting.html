Poetry Written in Porcelain

In England, Edmund de Waal is a celebrated potter, known for installations

of impeccably made vessels in soft shades of celadon or white, many of them

permanently displayed in places like the Victoria and Albert Museum here.

But in the United States Mr. de Waal is known primarily as a writer whose

2010 family memoir “The Hare With Amber Eyes” became a surprise best seller.

Despite countless rejections from publishers, it ended up selling more

than 1.5 million copies, has been translated into 26 languages and was recently

optioned for a movie.
